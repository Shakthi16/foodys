CHICKEN WITH GREEN VEG

Servings: 2-4
Prep time: 20 mins
Cooking time: 15 mins
Difficulty: Easy

METHOD

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add diced chicken breasts and cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add broccoli florets, snap peas, and green beans to the pan.
  4. Stir in minced garlic and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and stir to coat all ingredients evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
