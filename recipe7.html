MOJITO

Servings: 1
Prep time: 5 mins
Cooking time: 0 mins
Difficulty: Easy

METHOD

  1. Muddle the mint leaves and lime in a glass to release the mint oils and lime juice.
  2. Add the sugar and muddle again to mix.
  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  4. Pour the rum over the ice.
  5. Fill the rest of the glass with soda water.
  6. Stir well and garnish with additional mint leaves and a lime wedge.

INGREDIENTS

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 1/2 ounces white rum
  • 1/2 cup soda water
