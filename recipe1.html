SPECIAL SALAD CHICKEN

Servings: 2-4
Prep time: 20 mins
Cooking time: 15 mins
Difficulty: Easy

METHOD

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add diced chicken breasts and cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  4. Add the cooked chicken to the salad.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette and toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • Salt and pepper to taste
