NOODLE CHICKEN

Servings: 2-4
Prep time: 20 mins
Cooking time: 15 mins
Difficulty: Easy

METHOD

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add diced chicken breasts and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Add sliced bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and pour in soy sauce. Toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
