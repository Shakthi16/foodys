CHOCOLATE CAKE

Servings: 8-10
Prep time: 20 mins
Cooking time: 30 mins
Difficulty: Medium

METHOD

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin).
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  7. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1-3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
